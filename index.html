Safety Tips

Stay Alert

Always be aware of your surroundings. Avoid using your phone endlessly in isolated or poorly lit areas.

Share Location

Always share your live cab or transport location with a trusted friend or family member before traveling.

Keep Phone Charged

Ensure your mobile phone has enough battery and call credit before heading out.

Trust Instincts

If you feel uncomfortable or unsafe, leave the area immediately or move towards a populated place.

Self Defense

Carry pepper spray or know basic self-defense moves for emergencies. Be prepared to defend yourself.

Plan the Route

Know exactly where you are going and avoid unlit or deserted routes, especially late at night.
